## Typography
- Public guidance identifies Helvetica Now as the Crowe data-visualization typeface, with Helvetica Neue/Helvetica acceptable fallbacks in accessible template guidance.
- Implementation: system-installed `Helvetica Neue`, then `Helvetica`, then `Arial`, avoiding redistribution of proprietary font binaries.
- Character: direct, high-contrast, modern, disciplined hierarchy; large editorial headlines and compact technical labels.

## Visual grammar
- Crowe indigo and amber dominate.
- White and dark-indigo fields, dynamic angular direction, acute-path geometry, bold data stories, restrained secondary colors.
- No generic gradient background system. The site uses thin angled construction lines and an amber directional deck influenced by Crowe's documented SmartPath geometry without copying an official proprietary composition.
