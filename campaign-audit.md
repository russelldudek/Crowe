# Campaign audit

## Scope
Independent candidate vision for Russell Dudek and the Crowe Senior AI Solutions Consultant opportunity.

## Evidence integrity
- Candidate facts were drawn from the verified candidate evidence record and the supplied job description.
- Historical titles, dates, metrics, and claim strength were preserved.
- The campaign does not claim production ownership or platform tenure that is not supported by evidence.
- Low-code platform transferability is framed through architecture, workflow, governance, integration, and a bounded working-session approach rather than invented hands-on tenure.

**Evidence integrity: passed**

## Brand fidelity
- Visible Crowe wordmark appears above the fold and is stored locally.
- Crowe Indigo and Amber dominate the recognition layer.
- Color values are documented from the Crowe Digital Brand Hub.
- Typography uses licensed system fallbacks aligned to Crowe's published Helvetica guidance; no proprietary font files are shipped.
- The Outcome Span is candidate-created and remains distinct from Crowe's official identity.
- All public surfaces identify the work as an independent candidate vision.

**Brand fidelity: passed**  
**Visible company identity: passed**  
**Color token provenance: passed**  
**Typography decision: passed**  
**Committed brand assets: passed**  
**Document brand continuity: passed**  
**Independent-candidate distinction: passed**
